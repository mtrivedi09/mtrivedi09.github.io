Custom Autopilot Capabilities


Thr project is based on building a custom quadcopter, in which my study is focused on designing a part of the autopilot
that will control the quadcopter and execute the commands sent by the user. This will allow for the implementation of a
custom controller. The capability to implement custom controller will allow later, different theoretically proven control
strategies to be tested and supported with experimental results.

This research project was part of an independent study i took under Dr. Tansel Yucelen and a Phd student in the Laboratory
for Autonomy, Control, Information and Systems (lacis.eng.usf.edu) at University of South Florida.
